Refund Policy
At BSCIT WALLAH, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality web development services that meet and exceed our clients' expectations. We understand that circumstances might arise where a refund is requested. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions and procedures for requesting a refund.

Eligibility for Refunds:

1.1. Deposit for Projects: A non-refundable deposit is required before starting a project. This deposit secures your place in our development queue and covers initial administrative and planning work.

1.2. Project Milestone Payments: For projects that involve multiple milestones, refunds for milestone payments will be considered under the following conditions:

If the client cancels the project before the work for a specific milestone has begun, a refund for that milestone may be issued. Once work for a milestone has started, the payment for that milestone is non-refundable.

Refund Process:

2.1. Refund Request: To initiate a refund request, please contact our customer support team at Gmail and provide the relevant details, including the reason for the refund and any supporting documentation.

2.2. Approval and Processing: Refund requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Once approved, the refund will be processed within 48 hours. Refunds will be made using the original method of payment.

Non-Refundable Items and Services:

3.1. Custom Development Work: Payments for custom development work, including coding, design, and programming services, are non-refundable once the work has been initiated.

3.2. Hosting and Domain Fees: Hosting fees, domain registration fees, and related services are non-refundable as they are paid to third-party providers.

Project Cancellation:

4.1. If the client wishes to cancel a project after it has commenced, the portion of the project not completed will be assessed, and a partial refund may be considered based on the amount of work completed.

Dissatisfaction or Revisions:

5.1. If you are dissatisfied with the quality of the work, please contact us within 7 days of project completion. We will review the concerns and, if necessary, provide revisions to address the issues.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Refund Policy or wish to request a refund, please contact our customer support team at BSCIT WALLAH. We are here to address your inquiries and ensure a smooth resolution.

Please note that this Refund Policy is subject to change without prior notice. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically for any updates or modifications.

Thank you for choosing BSCIT WALLAH. We are committed to providing you with exceptional web development services and support.
